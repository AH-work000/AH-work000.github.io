Slot Racing Car Interactive Program

Remember those slot-car racing toys that you driven using handheld controllers? Now you can try it online in this Slot Racing Car Interactive Program! Try to use the virtual handheld controllers to race the two moon slot-cars across the circular race tracks. This interactive program is done as part of my major project for an introductory computer graphics subject. It replicates the realism of the slot-car racing toys under a dark environment with a lightsource positioned above in the computer graphics of the interactive program.

Mini Snowy Forest Generator

This simple web application allows you to randomly generate a variety of forest models: trees, dead trees, stumps, rockes and logs at different distinctive positions across a snowy plane. All of those models makes up a snowy forest. You have the options to configure the amount of forest models that is going to make up your snowy forest. There's also a refresh button to restart the forest generation if the resulting forest does not go to you liking.
